Adding, retiring, and never breaking history

Deactivate vs. delete · driven by whether any card has answered the question
The rule. A question can only be deleted while no scorecard has ever answered it. Once any card holds a value for it — even one draft — the question retires via Deactivate instead: it stops appearing on new scorecards but stays visible on every card that answered it, so committee history is never rewritten. is_system fields can be renamed, reweighted, or deactivated — never deleted. Status sits at the top of every card; its choice list is fully admin-editable here, and retired choices follow the same rule — hidden from the dropdown on new cards, still shown on cards that hold them.
